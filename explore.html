Explore: The Sewing Community

Resources

Getting involved, and integrating yourself into the sewing community can be a good idea for all beginners. Connecting with other people who share the same hobby as you can be motivation for you do go further, and can hold you accountable to finishing your projects. Being in a sewing community can also be very helpful to increasing your skillset quickly as you can learn from other sewers who have more experience. Joann Fabrics is a very good place to start your integration. They have weekly sewing, knitting, and crochet classes that are typically free. If you are nervous to try this, bringing a friend or family member can help push you to get started.
