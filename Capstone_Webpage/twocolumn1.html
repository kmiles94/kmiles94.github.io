Seattle Discards Web Map

Figure 1.7: This interactive web map displays 'show and hide layers' of Seattle's Sharps Discard Drop Boxes, along with discards that have been reported to the City of Seattle via their hotline since 2016. The data ends in early 2019. This map is meant to show how sharps discards in the public right of way have increase over the year. The installation of drop boxes is a pilot program Seattle Public Utilities (SPU) has put together to see if there is a reduction in discards in the future. If there is a reduction in discards, more drop boxes will be installed to neighborhoods that are affected the most. Please see Figure(s) 1.3, 1.4, 1.5 and 1.6 in the Neighborhoods tab of this webpage to see what neighborhoods are most affected.
